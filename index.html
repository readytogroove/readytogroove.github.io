HUMANS WITH PERIODS

A project to humanize periods with honest stories

Menstruation defies race, gender, ability, socioeconomic status, religion, culture, and geography. It is a vital part of the human condition, and individual experiences are vast, diverse, and unique. Humans with Periods aims to break down destructive taboos by sharing honest stories of humans just like you.
