Here’s why this info is important

Women are only able to get pregnant for a fraction of the menstrual cycle, but, sadly, this isn’t common knowledge. It’s simple to determine when exactly you’re fertile using something called the sympto-thermal method – sometimes referred to as the fertility awareness method, or FAM.

The links on this page are excerpts from The Cycle, a free ebook written by our CEO, Jennifer. These excerpts will teach you how to chart your fertility to avoid pregnancy, to maximize your chances of getting pregnant, or to monitor your reproductive health.
