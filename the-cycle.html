Here’s why we want to help

Periods are a majorly taboo topic, and as a result, many people don’t realize that the female reproductive system provides a live stream of valuable data every single day. This data can help you prevent pregnancy without hormones or devices, maximize your chances of getting pregnant, or help you improve and/or maintain your reproductive health.

We believe that education can change the world, which is why this information is provided to you free of charge. Happy learning!
