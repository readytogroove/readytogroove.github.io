The Cycle

Breaking Stigmas & Unlocking the Benefits of the Female Reproductive System with the Sympto-thermal Method of Fertility Awareness

The following excerpts from the book will teach you how to figure out exactly when you're fertile to naturally and effectively prevent pregnancy, to help you maximize your chances of getting pregnant quickly, or to help you keep tabs on your reproductive health. Our mission at Groove is to educate and empower women. That's why the entire book can be downloaded for free.
