Track your Cycle

Groove uses advanced algorithms to predict your period with incredible accuracy. The more you use Groove, and the more data you add, the smarter it will become.

Enter data about your unique cycle – bleeding, cervical fluid patterns, sexual behavior – and view it all on a stunning, one-of-a-kind chart. We work hard predicting your periods so you don’t have to.

Groove is the period tracker you’ve been waiting for – a beautifully simple app for a beautifully unique you.

Know when you’re fertile.

Many of us aren’t taught that women are only fertile for a fraction of the menstrual cycle. Groove will help you pinpoint these fertile days to practice the sympto-thermal method of fertility awareness to naturally and effectively avoid a pregnancy, to help you get pregnant, or to monitor and improve your reproductive health.

Enter data about two simple fertility signs – basal body temperature and cervical fluid – to practice the sympto-thermal method. Not sure where to start? We can help.
