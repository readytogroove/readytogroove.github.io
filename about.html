About the Groovers

Jennifer Aldoretta, Co-Groover & CEO

Jennifer studied mechanical engineering at Georgia Tech in Atlanta. Frustrated by the lack of education and innovation in women’s health, she was led to quit her job as an engineer in pursuit of a grand vision. She is passionate about the intersection of healthcare and technology and is devoted to transforming the status quo. She is committed to carrying out Groove’s mission because she believes it has the power to create widespread social change at a fundamental level by empowering women. When she’s not working or telling corny jokes, Jennifer enjoys cycling, running, traveling, reading, painting, and lounging with her dog Bailey.

Brian Smith, Co-Groover & CTO

Brian is an entrepreneur who studied mechanical engineering at Georgia Tech. He is a self-taught software engineer who specializes in Ruby on Rails, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and being awesome. He’s passionate about Groove because he believes women deserve knowledge and options when it comes to their healthcare, and he wants to build products that are well-designed and easy to use. In his free time, Brian enjoys running, reading, coding, biking, and doing just about anything outdoors.
