About the Groovers

Jennifer Aldoretta, Co-Groover & CEO

Jennifer studied mechanical engineering at Georgia Tech in Atlanta. Frustrated by the lack of innovation in birth control and conception tools, she was led to quit her job as an engineer in pursuit of a grand vision. She is passionate about the intersection of healthcare and technology and is devoted to transforming the status quo. When she's not working or telling corny jokes, Jennifer enjoys cycling, running, traveling, reading, painting, and lounging with her dog Bailey.

Brian Smith, Co-Groover & CTO

Brian is an entrepreneur who studied mechanical engineering at Georgia Tech. He is a self-taught software engineer who specializes in Ruby on Rails, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and being awesome. He's passionate about Groove because he believes apps for women should be as well-designed and easy to use as the best apps available. In his free time, Brian likes running, reading, coding, biking, and doing just about anything outdoors.
